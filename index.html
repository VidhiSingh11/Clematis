Get to Know Us


At Clematis, we believe everyone has the right to access mental health support and information when they need it. We launched our business out of a passion to provide a safe haven for people to explore their mental health and find the support they need. We understand that mental health can be a complex and lonely journey, but we are here to remind you that you are not alone. We strive to provide you with the right resources and tailored support to make your journey easier. Our team of mental health professionals are here to guide and support you every step of the way.
-Team Clematis
